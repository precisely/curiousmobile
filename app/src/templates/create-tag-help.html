Create a tracking tag
1
Enter your tag in the text box at the top of the screen.
2
Example tags:
migraine 5 7:14am (is it my pillow?)
run 34 min 9am (really humid today)

FORMAT:
[tag][amount*][unit*][timestamp*][(comment)*][repeat remind button*]
*optional

**Make sure you're on the 'Track' tab**



Type your tag in the box provided above, and then set repeat and alerts, or bookmark.



See Advanced Tracking for more information about how to make repeat, alert and bookmark tags, s well as setting a repeat frequency, and an end date.
